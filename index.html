What we do

Complete transportation
coordination, handled.

We work with vetted local NEMT providers to ensure every patient ride is scheduled, confirmed, and monitored — start to finish.

Ride scheduling & dispatch

We receive trip requests from your facility, assign the right vetted carrier, and confirm every pickup — so your staff never touches a phone for transportation.

Real-time ride monitoring

We track every trip live. If a driver is delayed, we handle the recovery immediately — before your patient or your team even knows there's an issue.

Recurring dialysis programs

Dialysis patients need rides 3 times a week, every week. We set up automated recurring schedules so nothing falls through the cracks and no-shows drop dramatically.

Vetted carrier network

Every transport partner in our network is screened for licensing, insurance, vehicle compliance, and on-time performance. We don't send unqualified drivers to your patients.

Simple billing & reporting

Clear invoicing on a weekly or monthly cycle. No surprise fees, no complicated billing codes. You get a clean record of every trip completed for your facility.

Ambulatory, wheelchair & gurney

We coordinate all transport types — ambulatory walkers, wheelchair-accessible vans, and gurney/stretcher vehicles — matched to each patient's specific medical needs.
